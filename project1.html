BRIEF
Design a new strategic opportunity for Swapfiets, a bike rental company.

DESIGN METHODS
desk research, service blueprint, customer journey map, strategic roadmap, future vision

SOLUTION
SwapFlex is a new subscription plan for Swapfiets. It enables users to rent bikes on demand in different cities, providing daily commuters with a more flexible option alongside the standard plan. It is the first milestone in a 10-year roadmap to expand the services portfolio offered by the company and establish itself as an integral part of people's lives.

PERSONAL CONTRIBUTION
First, I focused on trend research, specifically with regard to customers. I then oversaw the development of the service blueprint and the final visualisations of the customer journey, roadmap and the app.

PROBLEM FRAMING
The first phase of the design process involved analysing the company in order to identify internal issues and areas of potential. To this end, we researched market trends to understand customer values, created a positioning map, and recreated the company's operational framework, highlighting inefficiencies and opportunity spaces.
