BRIEF
Demonstrate, through theoretical reasons and on-the-field applications, why and how design is strategic.

DESIGN METHODS
literature review, desk research, brand analysis

ABSTRACT
This presentation explores the strategic value of design by outlining five core reasons why design plays a pivotal role in innovation and organizational development. Drawing on academic literature and the case of Swapfiets - a subscription-based bicycle service - the authors highlight how design enables future-oriented thinking, creates competitive differentiation, fosters user-centered innovation, connects organizations with users through creativity, and introduces flexibility within organizations. The work positions strategic design as a dynamic, integrative practice that not only challenges the status quo but also guides the co-creation of sustainable and impactful solutions for people, organizations, and the planet.
